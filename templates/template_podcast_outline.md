## Guidelines

### 3-Voice Balance:
- **Host:** 25% (questions, framing, transitions)
- **Support:** 30% (emotional context, examples, reset demos)
- **Technical:** 45% (mechanism breakdowns, architecture)

### Do:
- Start with relatable question
- Provide emotional context before mechanics
- Explain architecture clearly
- Debate tensions (don't avoid disagreement)
- Include story example
- Demo light reset
- Always end with CTA

### Don't:
- Lecture without dialogue
- Skip emotional reality
- Overcomplicate mechanisms
- Avoid the debate
- Forget the CTA

### Shamanic Integration:
- Technical emphasizes Steps 3, 4, 5
- Support demonstrates Steps 1, 7, 8
- Host ties it together with sovereignty themes
